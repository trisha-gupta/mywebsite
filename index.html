My Trip to Botswana!

A lot of people have been kind enough to follow my posts on social media about my latest trip to Botswana. So, I decided I should write about my experience (I am mostly into talking) with some pictures, therefore I will be adding a page on this website in the coming weeks about my trip, so stay tuned! For me, the travelling to Africa have always been a part of my reveries, a part of me lets loose and breaths in the fresh air reminicing all the beauty our planet has to offer. Botswana was truly spectacular, it is a little piece of paradise nestled in the heart of Africa, where one goes to rejuvenate and reconnect with themselves and nature. I yearn for the quietness, the care-free lifestyle and the way the sun set each evening. Thank you Botswana for touching my soul-one of the safest and happiest countries on the African continent and perhaps the world!
