Toronto, Chicago, California

Toronto

Growing up in Toronto was an amazing experience. Approximately 5 hours away from Ann Arbor, MI, Toronto is one of the most multicultural and diverse cities in the world. I was fortunate to have the privilidge to curate lifelong experiences in Toronto that have truly shaped my personality. In the summers, Toronto is a beautiful city with many outdoor activites. Similar to major cities in the US, Toronto is also a major tech hub in Canada and has the largest population in Canada. It is home to the CN Tower, and every major league sports team, primairly the Toronto Raptors for basketball, and the Toronto Maple Leafs for hockey!

Chicago

Since August of 2019, the Windy City has been my new home! Being a foodie, I have realized early on that it is a city with great food! In addition to its delicious food, there are so many things to enjoy. The sports teams, museums, bars, shopping, and beautiful buildings all add to its appeal. Summer is always so exciting and enjoyable here.

California

In January 2018 I took a trip to California. California is a magical place. There’s really no other state in the United States that has everything “Cali” does. From the spectacular mountains and wide windswept beaches to the glamor of Hollywood’s lights, California has a bit of everything for everyone. I lived in West Hollywood for my two week trip and here is a picture of me at the Santa Monica Pier.

New York, Isla Mujeres, Dominican Republic

New York

I have visited New York many times most recently in August 2018. It is truly a city that never sleeps! I love the shopping, culture and the hustle and bustle of Grand Central Station. It is a city where different people from all walks of life come together to carve out a life for themselves. It is disconnected, yet interconnected in so many ways. I have family across the city so over the years I have visted many boroughs including Queens, Brooklyn, Long Island and Harlem. I would say the Statue of Liberty has always been my favorite site to visit. There is also nothing like sitting and reading a book on a warm sunny day in Central Park.

Isla Mujeres

Isla Mujeres is found a few miles off the Caribbean coast, accessible via a short ferry ride from Cancun. I visited the island twice back to back. The first time in April 2018 and then again in May 2019 (I was awarded the trip with the company I was working with at the time). But in all reality, it almost feels like I was called back to the jewel of Mexico. The island is long and skinny — four miles in length but only 600 metres in width — and is a laid-back former fishing village, full of colourful buildings, surrounded by crystal clear waters, and a place where the primary method of transportation is a golf cart. An interesting fact is that in Spanish the name Isla Mujeres means Island of Women!

Dominican Republic

The Dominican Republic is the land with a rich collection of long history, the beautiful religion of bachata and merengue, incredible nature, and spectacular beaches with 50 shades of blue ocean. And all of that is in a reachable distance and with many different great places to discover. I visited Punta Cana at an all-inclusive resort in April 2016 and enjoyed every moment of it!

A mind that is stretched by a new experience can never go back to its old dimensions

Boston, My Sister+I, Key West

Boston

In November 2017, I visited the wonderful city of Boston. I have always been a big fan of Boston sports teams, especially the Red Sox and the Celtics. I had such a great time visiting many of the historical museums as well as two of the most prestigious universities in Harvard and MIT. The city was smaller than many other cities I have visited, but there were many beautiful views, and several great restaurants I was able to dine at. It was a very enjoyable experience.

My Sister+I

My sister and I are extremely close. She has truly been my best friend. We are 9 years apart and as I child I actually prayed for a younger sibling and I am glad that I did. She is my companion when I need one, best friend I can talk to and definitely the person who will always remind me when I am wrong! Thank you Kristal for coming into my life.
